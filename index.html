Kuzhimanna Service Co-operative Bank FAQ

Answers to the most searched questions from Kuzhimanna, Kizhisseri, and Malappuram customers.

What are the banking hours for Kuzhimanna Service Co-operative Bank?

Both the Kuzhimanna head office and Kadungallur branch operate Monday to Friday from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM and on Saturdays from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM, subject to Kerala State holidays.

How do I apply for a gold loan in Kuzhimanna or Kizhisseri?

Bring your gold ornaments, ID proof, and membership or passbook to either branch. Our team values and sanctions gold loans within 30 minutes with cooperative interest rates starting near 7%.

Does the bank support UPI, NEFT, and RTGS transfers?

Yes. Kuzhimanna Service Co-operative Bank offers IMPS/NEFT/RTGS at the branch and supports UPI collections wherever available. Check the digital banking section for timing updates.

Where can I find the IFSC code for Kuzhimanna Service Co-operative Bank?

The IFSC and MICR codes are printed on your passbook and cheque leaves. For the most current cooperative bank codes, please call 0483 2756104 or email kuzhimannascb@gmail.com before initiating transfers.
